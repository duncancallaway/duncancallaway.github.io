--- layout: home title: EMAC subtitle: Energy, Modeling, Analysis and Controls cover-img: [/assets/img/cover1.jpg] --- EMAC (Energy, Modeling, Analysis and Controls) is a group of scholars at UC Berkeley. We work on the engineering, techno-economic, and social challenges to decarbonizing electric power systems. We seek to develop and understand future pathways for decarbonization through energy systems analysis, and we also seek to solve some of the challenges those pathways present by developing new modeling, control, and optimization methods. We frequently collaborate with people in other organizations, including industry, government laboratories, and other academic institutions. EMAC alumni have gone into consulting, the private sector, government research, and academia. The group is led by Duncan Callaway.
